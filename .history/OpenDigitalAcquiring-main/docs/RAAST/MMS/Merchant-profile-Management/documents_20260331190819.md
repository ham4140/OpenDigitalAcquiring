# Merchant Documents

Merchant Documents refer to the various pieces of documentation that merchants must provide during the onboarding process. These documents are used to verify the merchant's identity, business operations, and financial details. The system supports the **uploading, validation, and storage** of these documents, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and business standards.

## **Document Upload Process**

MMP system provides an intuitive platform for merchants to securely upload all necessary documentation. The document upload process is streamlined to ensure that merchants can submit their documents in a timely manner without any confusion.

### **Supported Document Types**
The system supports uploading a wide range of document types, including but not limited to:

- **Personal Identification Documents**:
  - Government-issued IDs (e.g., Passport, National ID, Driver’s License)
  - Selfie with ID (for identity verification)
  
- **Business Registration Documents**:
  - Articles of Incorporation
  - Business Registration Certificates
  - Trade License

- **Financial Documents**:
  - Recent Bank Statements
  - IBAN and Account Proof Documents
  - Proof of Funds

- **Address Verification Documents**:
  - Utility Bills (e.g., electricity, water, internet)
  - Lease Agreement

- **Tax Documents**:
  - Tax Identification Number (TIN) document
  - Tax Returns or Certificates

### **Document Upload Features**
- **Multi-Document Upload**: The system allows merchants to upload multiple documents at once to streamline the process. Each document type is categorized to ensure that the right documents are uploaded for the correct step.
  
- **Secure Uploads**: All documents uploaded to the system are encrypted using industry-standard security protocols to ensure confidentiality and integrity of the data.

- **Real-Time Validation**: Once documents are uploaded, they are automatically checked for compliance with predefined standards. If a document is missing required information or is not in the correct format, the system will notify the user to take corrective action.

- **Document Review and Approval**: Once the documents are uploaded, they enter the review stage. The **Checker**, **Banker**, and **Approver** can access these documents to verify the authenticity of the information provided during the onboarding process.

### **Document Formats Supported**
The system supports a variety of file formats for document upload, including:

- PDF (.pdf)
- JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
- PNG (.png)
- TIFF (.tiff)
- Word Documents (.docx)

### **Document Retention and Security**
- **Secure Storage**: All uploaded documents are securely stored in a centralized, encrypted database. Access to the documents is strictly controlled and only accessible by authorized personnel.
  
- **Retention Policies**: The system allows for configurable document retention policies based on regulatory requirements. Documents can be stored for as long as needed and securely deleted when no longer required.


## **KYC Document Verification**

As part of the **KYC** process, merchants must submit various documents that the system verifies for accuracy. The following checks are applied to the uploaded documents:

1. **Identity Verification**: Ensures the personal ID documents match the merchant's details (e.g., name, date of birth).
2. **Business Verification**: Confirms that the business registration document is valid and corresponds to the business name and registration number provided.
3. **Banking Details Verification**: Ensures that the uploaded bank account documents are legitimate and correspond to the account details provided.
4. **Address Verification**: Verifies that the business address provided matches the address on the uploaded document (e.g., utility bill).


## **Configurable Document Upload Process**

Our system offers a flexible and configurable document upload process based on merchant types and regional regulations:

- **Custom Document Categories**: The document upload process can be customized to include different types of documents based on the merchant's industry or geographic location.
- **Role-Based Access to Documents**: Only authorized users (e.g., **Maker**, **Checker**, **Approver**) can view and verify specific documents during the onboarding process.
- **Multi-Language Support**: The document upload and validation process can be configured to support multiple languages to cater to global merchant bases.
